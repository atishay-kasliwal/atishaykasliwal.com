# 🔍 How to Find Cloudflare Pages

If you don't see "Workers & Pages" in the left sidebar, the navigation might have changed. Try these locations:

## Navigation Options

### Option 1: Left Sidebar
Look in the left sidebar for:
- **"Pages"** (might be its own menu item)
- **"Workers"** (Pages might be inside this)
- **"Website"** or **"Websites"**
- **"Apps"**

### Option 2: Top Navigation
Check the top navigation bar for:
- **"Products"** → Then look for Pages
- **"Explore Products"**
- **"More"**

### Option 3: Direct URL
Try going directly to:
- **https://dash.cloudflare.com/pages**
- **https://dash.cloudflare.com/workers**

### Option 4: Search
Most Cloudflare dashboards have a search box. Type:
- **"Pages"**
- **"Workers & Pages"**
- **"Deploy"**

### Option 5: Account Homepage
On your main Cloudflare dashboard:
1. Look for **"Get Started"** or **"Create"** buttons
2. Look for **"Quick Actions"** or **"Quick Links"**
3. Look for icons representing websites or deployments

## Where to Look in Your Current View

You mentioned you're on the **DNS Records** page. Try:

1. **Click "Overview"** in the left sidebar (you're currently on "DNS")
2. Look for **"Pages"** in the left sidebar
3. Or scroll down on the left sidebar to find **"Workers"**

## Alternative: Create from Domain Menu

If you can't find Pages directly, try:

1. On your domain overview page (where it shows "Active")
2. Look for tabs at the top: **"Overview" | "Analytics" | "Security" | "SSL/TLS" | "Speed" etc.**
3. See if there's a **"Pages"** or **"Deploy"** tab

## Cloudflare Account Types

Make sure you're logged into the correct Cloudflare account:
- Free accounts have **Pages** available
- Some account types might limit access
- Check you're on the account that has your domain (atishaykasliwal.com)
