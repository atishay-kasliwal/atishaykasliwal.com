## 🚀 Make Google Index Your Site (15 Minutes)

### Step 1: Submit to Google Search Console

1. Go to: **https://search.google.com/search-console**
2. Click **"Start now"** or **"Add property"**
3. Enter your domain: **`atishaykasliwal.com`**
4. Verify ownership (choose one method):

   **Option A: DNS Verification (Easiest)**
   - Click "Domain" property type
   - Copy the TXT record Google gives you
   - Go to Cloudflare → DNS → Add Record
   - Type: TXT
   - Name: @
   - Content: [paste the TXT record]
   - Click Save
   - Back in Google, click "Verify"

   **Option B: HTML File Upload**
   - Click "URL prefix" property type
   - Download the HTML verification file
   - I'll help you add it to your site

5. Once verified, you're done!

### Step 2: Submit Your Sitemap

After verification:
1. In Search Console, click **"Sitemaps"** in the left sidebar
2. Enter: `https://atishaykasliwal.com/sitemap.xml`
3. Click **"Submit"**
4. Wait a few seconds, should say "Success"

### Step 3: Request Indexing

Help Google find your homepage faster:
1. In Search Console, click **"URL Inspection"**
2. Enter: `https://atishaykasliwal.com`
3. Click **"Request Indexing"**
4. Repeat for other pages if desired:
   - `https://atishaykasliwal.com/art`
   - `https://atishaykasliwal.com/projects`
