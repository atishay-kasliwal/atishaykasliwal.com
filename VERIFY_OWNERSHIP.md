# Complete Ownership Verification

## What You See Now:
✅ Nameservers updated to Cloudflare
⏳ Domain status: PENDING (needs ownership verification)

## What to Do:

1. **Go to the Overview Page**
   - Click the **"Overview"** link in the orange banner on the DNS page
   - OR go to the "Overview" tab in the left sidebar

2. **Complete Verification Steps**
   - Cloudflare will show you steps to verify domain ownership
   - This usually involves:
     - Adding a TXT record to verify ownership, OR
     - Confirming nameserver changes are complete
     - Waiting a few minutes for DNS propagation

3. **Wait for Status Change**
   - The orange "pending" banner will disappear
   - Domain status will change to "Active"
   - You'll see a success message

## After Verification:
Once verified and status changes to "Active":
1. Your DNS will be fully managed by Cloudflare
2. SSL certificate will provision (takes 5-15 minutes)
3. Your site will be accessible at atishaykasliwal.com
